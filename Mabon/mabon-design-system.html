Mabon Design System

A comprehensive design system for the Autumn Equinox celebration invitation. Built with warmth, balance, and community in mind—honoring the second harvest and the sacred equilibrium between light and dark.

WCAG Contrast Guidelines

  • ✓ Body text: Use Deep Red (#8B2942), Forest (#2D4A3E), or Earth (#6B4423) on Cream (#F7F3ED) — minimum 4.5:1 ratio
  • ✓ Large text: Gold (#D4A853) on Cream achieves 3:1 ratio for headlines
  • ✓ Buttons: White text on Deep Red or Forest backgrounds

Welcome to Mabon

As the wheel of the year turns, we find ourselves at the autumnal equinox— a moment of perfect balance between light and dark. This is Mabon, the second harvest, a time to give thanks for what we've grown and release what no longer serves us.

What to Expect

Our celebration will include:

  • A gratitude ritual to honor the season's abundance
  • Reflection exercises for balancing light and shadow
  • Seasonal refreshments and community connection
  • A symbolic "letting go" ceremony
"The equinox invites you to stand in between two forces—light and shadow, growth and decay—and find where you belong in that moment."

Come as you are. Bring an item to place on the gratitude altar, and leave with a heart full of connection. ✨
