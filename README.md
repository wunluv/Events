# Beeja Mara Events

> The Events arm of our Intentional Community startup — coordinating gatherings, rituals, celebrations, and meaningful moments that bind our community together.

## Overview

This repository serves as the central hub for all event-related coordination within our Intentional Community. From seasonal celebrations to weekly gatherings, this is where we plan, document, and reflect on the moments that matter.

## What We Do

- **Seasonal Celebrations** — Honoring the wheel of the year (Sabats and Equinoxes)
- **Community Gatherings** — Regular meetups, circles, and collective practices
- **Ritual Coordination** — Organizing meaningful ceremonies and rites of passage
- **Event Documentation** — Capturing the essence of our gatherings through photos, notes, and reflections

## Current Events

### Mabon — The Autumn Equinox
*Mabon* marks the second harvest and the balance of light and dark as we transition into the darker half of the year. Explore the [Mabon](./Mabon/) directory to see our recent Equinox celebration.
